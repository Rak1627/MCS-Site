    about company

    10+ Years of
    experience in Australian education industry

    Maven Consulting Group, founded in 2018, provides a comprehensive solution to students who are looking to pursue higher education and vocational training in Australia. With more than 10 years of experience in the Australian International Education industry, we aim to provide excellent customer service and extensive guidance to our clients. We have our branches in Australia (Melbourne and Tasmania), Nepal, Mongolia, Philippines and Brazil.

    We believe international education shall provide students with quality education, a global perspective, and international exposure catalyzing their personal and professional growth. Hence, with the help of our experts, we aim to promote internationally recognized educational institutes of Australia to international students and help them earn an internationally recognized degree.
